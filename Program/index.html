SPLASH Event Program

The program runs across two main stages and workshops in parallel throughout the day.

  • Stage A – Keynotes & Pitching: Inspiring talks from founders, investors, and the SPLASH Pitching Competition.
  • Stage B – Panels & Discussions: Engaging conversations on entrepreneurship, innovation, and ecosystem trends.
  • Workshops – Hands-On Learning: Practical sessions with skill-building, exercises, and insights.

Join SPLASH Matchmaking via QR code at the venue to connect instantly with investors, founders, and peers. The schedule balances deep-dive expertise, beginner-friendly sessions, and networking opportunities throughout the day.
